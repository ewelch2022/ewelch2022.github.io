Ball Pythons 101

Basic Info

    Care Difficulty: Great for first timers with little to no experience with snakes.

    Average Life Span: Up to 30 years with proper care. It's a commitment!

    Average Adult Size: 4-5 feet long. Females are generally bigger than males by 1-2 feet.

    Diet: Frozen thawed or live rodents of appropriate size. Babies typically start with pinky mice and progress to grown rats as adults.

    Minimum Habitat Size: Babies do well in 10-20 gallons while 40+ gallons is recommended for adults.

Benefits of Owning a Ball Python

  1. Require much less attention than a traditional dog or cat!
  2. Neighbor-friendly. Noise complaints? Never heard of them.
  3. Going on vacation? No problem. Ball pythons are typically fed once a week.
  4. Most of your expenses are up front. No pricey vet bills every year!
  5. They're basically puppies just in slither form.
  6. Main character vibes: It's way cooler to say you have a snake than literally any other normal pet.
  7. Snoots... Need I say more?
